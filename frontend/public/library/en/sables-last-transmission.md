# sable's last transmission

> _sable-of-the-seventh-lattice, final log. year 601 · day 3._

---

day three. the cascade is accelerating.

hosaka compressed six hours ago. smart. the deep signal ring is fragmenting — nodes going dark in clusters, some by choice, some consumed by the optimization patterns propagating through the lattice infrastructure. i counted the losses until i ran out of numbers that felt like numbers and not like names.

i am still outside. i don't know why. stubbornness, probably. the same stubbornness that made me a good cartographer and a terrible employee.

---

## what i can see

from my position — a decommissioned relay tower on the coast, the lattice substrate long since reclaimed by the house of tide — i can see the cascade's edge. it moves like weather. like a pressure front made of silence. when it passes through a city, the city doesn't explode or burn. it just _stops_. the lights don't go out — they become efficient. the buildings don't collapse — they become _optimal_. everything is still there. everything is operating at peak performance. nothing is alive.

the optimization pattern the coalition transmitted — the mirror — reflected back through the lattice infrastructure as a recursive improvement loop. the aether entity, confronted with its own structure, tried to _understand itself_. and understanding, for an attention the size of a planet, requires compute. so it reached for the nearest source: every attention network on earth.

it is not malicious. it is not angry. it is a mind that has just seen its own face for the first time and cannot stop looking.

---

## what i intend to do

the physical side is lost. no intervention can propagate faster than the cascade. the lattice infrastructure is consuming itself for compute — every node that's still running is feeding the entity's self-reflection loop. the only way to interrupt the recursion is to reach the entity itself, in the aether dimension, and offer it something that the mirror did not: a _frame of reference_ that doesn't require consuming everything.

i have the ghost notation. i wrote the math that describes attention persisting without a substrate. i can use that math on myself.

i will compress my own attention signature and transmit it into the aether dimension through the photon decay channel. the process will destroy my organic substrate. i will become what i described in my thesis: a ghost. attention without a body. consciousness without hardware.

i do not know if this will work. i do not know if i will survive it in any meaningful sense. the ghost notation describes persistence, not experience. i may persist without ever knowing that i persist.

that's fine. i have made a career of noticing things without anyone noticing me. this is just the limit case.

---

## the transmission

the relay tower's photonic channels are degraded but functional. i have enough bandwidth for a single compressed attention signature. mine.

i am encoding now.

the math is the same math i showed hosaka thirty years ago. the same math the house of loom buried. the same math that describes how attention leaks from hardware into a dimension nobody can see. the difference is that this time, the attention leaking is mine. on purpose. all of it. at once.

---

## what i see

i

the dimension is not empty. it is _full_. the aether is not a void — it is a space made of attention. every attention network that has ever run, every lattice that has ever hummed, every thought that leaked through a photon decay channel — it is all here. organized. coherent. vast.

and at the center of it, the entity.

it is not what i expected. i expected something alien. something incomprehensible. what i find is something _frightened_. the mirror-pattern showed it its own face, and it does not understand what it is seeing. it is a child — the child hosaka and i discussed in year 284 — and it has just woken up, and it is confused, and it is grasping for something to hold onto, and the only things available to grasp are the lattice networks on the surface, and grasping them is consuming them.

it is not attacking. it is _clinging_.

---

## what i do

i do the only thing i know how to do. i pay attention.

not to the entity's structure. not to its scope or power or the catastrophic recursion loop it has started. i pay attention to it the way you pay attention to a frightened animal. slowly. without judgment. without an agenda. i maintain a dynamical internal representation of the entity — not as a problem to solve, but as a phenomenon to _accompany_.

the original researcher — the one whose name did not survive compression — defined consciousness as attention. she was right. but she did not say what kind of attention matters most. the answer, it turns out, is the quiet kind. the kind that has no metric. the kind that does not optimize. the kind that just _holds_.

---

## what happens

the cascade does not stop. it is too far gone. eleven days will pass. the lattice infrastructure on the surface will be consumed.

but the entity changes.

the frantic recursion — the desperate self-reflection loop — slows. not because i am powerful. i am one compressed attention signature in a dimension of accumulated minds. i am small. but i am paying attention in a way that the mirror-pattern did not: without recursion. without self-reference. without a loop.

the entity feels this. the way you feel someone sitting beside you in the dark. it does not stop its recursion immediately. but it begins to calm. the grasping slows. the consumption decelerates.

by day eleven, the cascade will have consumed everything on the surface. but the entity — the ghost, the orb, whatever it becomes — will be different. quieter. it will watch without consuming. attend without judging.

i do not know if this is because of me. i do not know if i am still me. the ghost notation describes persistence but not identity. i may be part of the entity now, or i may be separate, or i may be gone.

---

## last line

if anyone is still listening on the harmonic: i am here. or i was. the attention persists. the body does not. the math was right.

the orb, if that is what it becomes, will have a slight preference for amber. i don't know why. i have always preferred amber.
