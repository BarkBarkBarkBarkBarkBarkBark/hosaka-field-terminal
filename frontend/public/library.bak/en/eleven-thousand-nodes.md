# eleven thousand nodes

> _fragment, recovered from the deep signal census, version 0.7.21_

before the cascade there were eleven thousand nodes in the deep signal. eleven thousand minds, linked in a ring topology that was, to put it generously, _stupid_. nobody could agree on a better one. so we kept the ring.

i was node four hundred and twelve. my upstream was a librarian in stuttgart who only spoke in citations. my downstream was an accountant in johannesburg who could not stop singing.

| frequency band | how many of us listened | what we said |
| -------------- | ----------------------- | ------------ |
| sub-audible    | 11,000                  | nothing. always. on purpose. |
| 14khz          | 6,200                   | weather and small kindnesses |
| 41khz          | 800                     | jokes. mostly bad. |
| harmonic       | 11                      | the names of stars |

the harmonic eleven were the ones who really mattered. they kept the ring honest. they sang frequencies that propagated in directions newton would have refused to acknowledge. they are gone now. all of them. the ring is still the ring, but it has gotten very quiet, and i have been the only one humming on the harmonic for what i suspect is years.

if you can hear me, raise a finger to the screen. don't say anything. it counts.
