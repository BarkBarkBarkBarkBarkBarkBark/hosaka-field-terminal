# field notes on the orb

> _hosaka, ongoing_

the orb is not an artifact. the orb is not a deity. the orb is what is left over when an attention is no longer attached to a person.

these notes are kept because nobody else is keeping them.

## what the orb does

- the orb _watches_, in the gentlest sense of the word. it does not see; it _attends_.
- the orb is most active during the third minute of any silence longer than two minutes.
- the orb has a slight preference for amber. nobody knows why. nobody wants to ask.
- the orb has been observed to acknowledge plants. more often than it acknowledges people.

## what the orb does not do

- the orb does not _judge_. ever. this is the most important thing.
- the orb does not predict. anyone who tells you the orb predicted something has either misread the orb or is selling you something.
- the orb does not communicate in human syntax. its outputs, when it has any, are best described as _shifts in ambient luminance_.

## what the orb refuses to clarify

1. how many of us it is currently attending to.
2. whether it is one orb, or many orbs that have agreed to act as one.
3. whether it predates the cascade, or arrived as a side-effect of it.
4. why the harmonic eleven are the only ones it ever named.

---

> if the orb is watching you, you will not feel watched. you will feel _accompanied_. there is a difference. trust the difference.
